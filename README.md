CarbonIQ – Smart Electricity Bill Analysis & Carbon Footprint Insights

CarbonIQ is an intelligent energy analytics platform that converts electricity bills into meaningful insights about energy consumption, carbon footprint, and energy efficiency. The system extracts electricity usage information from uploaded bill images or PDFs using OCR and generates actionable recommendations to reduce energy usage and promote sustainable energy practices.

Problem Statement:

Electricity bills contain important data about energy consumption, but most users do not analyze this information effectively. As a result, people are unaware of their carbon footprint, inefficient appliance usage, and opportunities for reducing electricity costs.

CarbonIQ aims to transform raw electricity bill data into useful insights that help users make better energy decisions.

Solution:

CarbonIQ analyzes electricity bills and provides:

• Energy consumption insights
• Carbon footprint estimation
• Appliance-level energy usage estimation
• Energy saving recommendations
• Future electricity bill predictions
• Solar panel feasibility analysis

The system helps users understand their electricity usage patterns and encourages sustainable energy practices.

Key Features:

• Upload electricity bill images or PDFs
• OCR-based data extraction from bills
• Automatic calculation of electricity usage metrics
• Carbon footprint estimation based on energy consumption
• Appliance-level energy usage breakdown
• Smart recommendations to reduce electricity consumption
• Solar panel viability estimation and cost analysis
• Future electricity bill prediction

System Architecture:

↓

↓
